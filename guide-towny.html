Your First 15 Minutes

  1. Find wilderness — Walk until chat says "Wilderness" or use /towny map
  2. Create town — /town new YourName ($200, check with /balance)
  3. Set spawn — Stand somewhere safe, /town set spawn
  4. Claim surrounding chunks — /town claim auto 3 (claims 3 chunks outward)
  5. Invite friends — /town invite PlayerName (they type /town accept)
  6. Set basic permissions — /town set perm outsider build off (stops randoms)
  7. Deposit money — /town deposit 500 to cover first few days of upkeep

1. Creating a Town

Requirements: $200 in your personal balance. Stand in the chunk you want as your town center (homeblock). Must be at least 4 chunks away from any other town's homeblock.

/town new YourTown Creates town where you stand
/town set spawn Sets your town's return point
/town set board [message] Sets town message
/town View town info and stats

After creation: You're now mayor. Your town starts with 8 claim blocks (enough for 8 chunks) and can invite unlimited residents. Every new resident adds 10 claim blocks to your total capacity.

2. Claiming Land: How to Get More Chunks

There are FIVE ways to increase your town's claim limit:

Method 1: Residents (Base Claims)

Each resident in your town contributes 10 claim blocks to your total capacity. This is your baseline. More residents = more land you can claim.

Method 2: Nation Bonuses

Being part of a nation grants bonus claim blocks based on nation size. Larger nations give bigger bonuses.

  • Small nation (3-5 towns): +50 claim blocks
  • Medium nation (6-10 towns): +150 claim blocks
  • Large nation (11+ towns): +300 claim blocks

Use /nation to check your nation's current bonus.

Method 3: Milestone Rewards (Staff-Reviewed)

As your town grows and builds, you can apply for milestone reviews in Discord. Staff evaluate your town sequentially from M1 upward — you must complete earlier milestones before applying for later ones. Each milestone grants bonus claim blocks.

MilestoneRewardRequirements
M1+150 blocks 10 standard buildings · 1 large building
M2+300 blocks 20 standard buildings · 1 large building · 1 specialty building
M3+500 blocks 30 standard buildings · 3 large buildings · 2 specialty buildings (different types)
M4+700 blocks 40 standard buildings · 3 large buildings · 3 specialty buildings (all distinct)
M5+1200 blocks 50 standard buildings · 4 large buildings · Capital building (town hall, parliament, etc. — does not count as specialty)
Building Definitions:
  • Standard: 8×8 minimum footprint, fully enclosed (walls + roof)
  • Large: 16×16 minimum (1 full chunk), visually prominent
  • Specialty: 8+ chunks total, civic/military/economic/industrial/maritime function, visually distinct. Examples: Port, Armoury, University, Grand Market, Cathedral. Factories and Capital buildings excluded.
Port Lighthouse Shipyard Armoury Barracks Training Grounds Cathedral University Grand Market Bank Refinery Engineering Hall

How to apply: Open a Discord support ticket and request milestone evaluation. Staff will check your builds and award all completed milestones at once.

Method 4: Weekly Rankings

Every week, the server runs ranking events (check Discord for details). Winning towns receive bonus claim blocks as prizes. Amounts vary weekly — participate to earn extra land!

Method 5: Purchasing Bonus Claims

You can directly buy additional claim blocks using town money:

/town buy bonus [#] Purchase bonus claim blocks

Cost scales with each purchase. Check current price with /town.

The Claiming Process Itself

Once you have claim blocks available, claiming chunks costs money from your town bank:

  • First chunk: $5.00
  • Each subsequent claim: +2% more than previous
  • Maximum cost: $175 per chunk
  • Unclaim refund: 15% of original cost
/town claim Claim current chunk
/town claim auto [num] Claim multiple chunks outward
/town unclaim Remove claim (refunds 15%)
/town deposit [amt] Add money for claiming

Distance rules: Your homeblock has a 4-chunk protection buffer — no one can claim within 4 chunks of it. Towns in the same nation can claim directly adjacent (no buffer).

3. Permissions (Who Can Build/Fight)

Groups: resident (town members), nation, ally, outsider

Actions: build, destroy, switch (buttons/doors/levers), itemuse, pvp

/town set perm outsider build off Stop randoms building
/town set perm resident pvp off No friendly fire
/town set perm ally switch on Allies can use doors

Outlaws

/town outlaw add [player] — They can't enter your town, can be attacked anywhere inside, and are jailed for 2 hours if killed here.

4. Taxes & Upkeep

Daily upkeep: $2 per claimed chunk, paid from town bank at midnight EST. If bank hits $0, town enters bankruptcy and is deleted after 7 days.

Overclaiming penalty: If you claim more land than your resident count allows, pay $100 per extra chunk per day.

Income Sources

/town set tax [amount] Per-resident daily tax (max $1k)
/town set plottax [amount] Tax on claimed plots
/town deposit [amount] Add personal money

5. Plot Types

/plot set type [type] — Changes current chunk's purpose:

  • shop — Commercial district (blue C on map)
  • arena — PvP always on
  • embassy — $5k cost, allows foreign teleport (requires staff approval)
  • jail — Prison cells (see jail section)
  • farm — Agricultural zone
  • wilds — Harvest only, no building
  • inn — Visitors can set bed here

6. Nations

Join: /nation join [name] — Request to join existing nation.

Create: Need 5 residents, $10k in town bank. /nation new [name]

Benefits: Nation chat (/nc), share borders, claim bonuses, mutual defense.

7. Jail System

Set a jail plot with /plot set type jail. When outlaws or enemies die in your town, they're automatically jailed here.

  • Setup cost: $1,000 one-time
  • Prisoner cost: $250 per hour (paid by town)
  • Bail amounts: $1k standard / $5k mayor / $10k king
  • Max sentence: 12 hours

Note: Jailed players can still use ender pearls — build cells accordingly.

8. Embassy System (Cross-Nation Travel)

Since you can't directly teleport to towns outside your nation, embassies are the only way to establish fast travel between different nations.

Process:

  1. Travel to target town (physically)
  2. They set a plot to embassy: /plot set type embassy (costs them $5,000)
  3. Build an embassy structure (government-style building)
  4. Apply for staff approval in Discord with screenshots
  5. Once approved, use /town outpost to teleport there ($500, 1h cooldown)

1 Creating a Town

Establishing your first settlement requires strategic planning and capital. Town creation costs and requirements are based on server configuration.

Requirements & Costs

Creation Cost$200 (config: economy.new_expand.price_new_town)
Minimum Distance4 chunks from any existing town's homeblock (config: claiming.distance_rules.min_distance_from_town_homeblock)
LocationMust be in unclaimed territory
Default Tax ModePercentage-based (10% default tax)

Creation Command

/town new <TownName>
Creates a new town at your current location ($200)

Initial Town Benefits

  • Base Claim Limit: 8 townblocks (town_level: 0 residents tier)
  • Bonus Block Purchases: Up to 20 additional blocks can be purchased (town_level: 1 resident tier)
  • Default Public Status: True (town.default_public)
Strategic tip: Choose a location near resources but defensible. Check available funds with /balance before creating.
Requirement: You must be in an unclaimed area at least 4 chunks from any existing town's homeblock. Towns with 0 residents cannot claim outposts.

4 Taxes & Upkeep

Daily taxes and upkeep are collected each "Towny day" (config: plugin.day_timer.day_interval: 24h).

Tax Commands

/town set tax <amount>
Sets daily tax (max $1,000 or 15% with $10,000 cap)
/town set plottax <amount>
Sets tax for individual plot owners (max $1,000)
/town withdraw <amount>
Withdraws money from town bank
/town deposit <amount>
Deposits money to town bank

Tax Limits

Tax TypeMaximum
Flat Town Tax$1,000 per resident
Percentage Town Tax15% of resident balance (max $10,000 per payment)
Plot Tax$1,000 per plot
Nation Tax (flat)$1,000 per town
Nation Tax (percentage)15% (max $10,000 per town)

Upkeep Calculation

Base Town Upkeep$15.00 daily (config: economy.daily_taxes.price_town_upkeep)
Upkeep TypePlot-based: number of claimed plots × $15.00
Overclaim Penalty$100 per plot over limit (config: economy.daily_taxes.price_town_overclaimed_upkeep_penalty)
Penalty CalculationPlot-based: (plots over limit × $100)
Nation Upkeep$1,000 daily (config: economy.daily_taxes.price_nation_upkeep)

Bankruptcy System

If a town cannot pay upkeep, it enters bankruptcy. After 7 days bankrupt, chunks are unclaimed and town is deleted (config: economy.bankruptcy.enabled).

Warning: Towns that cannot pay upkeep enter bankruptcy. After 7 days at debt cap, the town will be deleted (economy.bankruptcy.debt_cap.allowed_days: 7).
Strategy: Balance taxes to cover upkeep without driving residents away. Use /town to check daily costs and current balance.

5 Town Permissions

Control what players can do within your town's borders.

Permission Command

/town set perm <who> <type> <on/off>
Sets permissions for groups (resident, nation, ally, outsider)

Default Town Permissions

GroupBuildDestroySwitchItem Use
Residents✅ ON✅ ON✅ ON✅ ON
Nation Members❌ OFF❌ OFF❌ OFF❌ OFF
Allies❌ OFF❌ OFF❌ OFF❌ OFF
Outsiders❌ OFF❌ OFF❌ OFF❌ OFF

Default Town Flags

  • PVP: ON (default)
  • Fire: OFF
  • Explosions: OFF
  • Mobs: OFF

Outlaw System

  • Outlaws can always be harmed in towns they're outlawed in (force_pvp_on_outlaws: true)
  • Outlaws cannot enter towns where outlawed (allow_outlaws_to_enter_town: false)
  • Outlaws cannot use teleport items (Ender Pearls/Chorus Fruit) while outlawed
  • Outlaws are teleported away after 5 seconds if found in town
Common configurations:
/town set perm outsider build off — Prevent outsiders building
/town set perm resident pvp off — Disable PvP between residents

Wilds Plot (Special Resource Type)

Wilds plots allow harvesting of natural resources while preventing building. Allowed blocks include:

  • Ores: All overworld and nether ores
  • Logs: All wood types
  • Plants: Flowers, mushrooms, crops
  • Other: Clay, glowstone, vines, cocoa

Plot Taxes

  • Plot taxes are collected daily along with town taxes
  • If a resident cannot pay plot tax, the plot becomes town land (config: town.default_taxes.does_non-payment_place_plot_for_sale: false)
  • Embassy plots have default 10% tax rate
Zoning strategy: Create commercial districts near spawn, wilds plots for resource gathering on the outskirts, and embassy plots near borders for diplomatic relations.

11 Embassy System

Since outposts are disabled and cross-nation teleportation is restricted, the Embassy System is the ONLY way to establish teleport links between towns in different nations.

How Embassy Teleportation Works

Towns cannot teleport to other towns outside their nation—even allies must use the embassy system. To establish a teleport link:

1 Travel to target town — Physically journey to the town you want to establish an embassy with.
2 Purchase embassy plot — The recipient town must have an embassy plot available. Cost: $5,000 creation fee (paid by recipient when setting plot type). Default tax: 10%.
3 Build embassy structure — Construct a government-style building on the embassy plot that looks appropriate for diplomatic purposes.
4 Apply in Discord — Submit your embassy build for approval. Include screenshots and coordinates.
5 Staff review — Once approved, staff will configure the embassy as a valid teleport destination.

Embassy Plot Specifications

Creation Cost$5,000 (config: townblocktypes.types.embassy.cost)
Default Tax10% (town.default_taxes.embassy_tax)
Map KeyE (visible on /towny map)
Command/plot set type embassy (must be run by recipient town)

Teleportation Rules

  • Only your nation: /town spawn works only for towns in your own nation
  • Allies cannot teleport: Even allied nations must use embassies
  • Embassy teleport: Once approved, your town can teleport directly to your embassy in the foreign town
  • No outposts: Traditional outpost claiming is disabled

Embassy Benefits

  • Establish diplomatic presence in foreign cities
  • Quick travel between allied nations without walking
  • Safe haven in foreign territory (embassy plots retain your build permissions)
  • Visible symbol of international relations
Critical: Without an approved embassy, you CANNOT teleport to towns outside your nation—even if they're allied. Plan your diplomatic infrastructure accordingly.
Strategy: Major trading hubs should establish embassies with key partner towns. Consider a "embassy district" near your town spawn for foreign delegations.

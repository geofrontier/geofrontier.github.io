Apply for Staff

Join our volunteer team and help shape the GeoFrontier community

Application Information

Be thoughtful in your responses: We're looking for dedicated community members who want to help others. Quality responses show us who you are and why you'd be a good fit.

Be specific about availability: Include your timezone and typical hours. This helps us schedule the voice call and understand when you'd be available to help.

Experience matters: Mention any relevant experience, both within and outside Minecraft. Even non-moderation experience can be valuable.

Show your personality: The "tell us about yourself" question is your chance to stand out. We want to know who you are, not just what you can do.

Application Process

  • Submission: Complete and submit the form below
  • Review: Our lead mods or admins will review your application
  • Decision: You'll be reached out to with a decision
  • Voice Call: If accepted, we'll schedule a quick VC to get to know you
  • Onboarding: You'll be shown around the team and trained

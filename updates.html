Towny Economy Overhaul

2026/02/06 ShilhohTony Towny Update
  • Creation Costs: Town creation set to $250, Nation creation $5000
  • Claim System: Base claim cost $5/chunk, increases by 5% per chunk, caps at $175/chunk
  • Claim Limits: 10 x # of residents chunks available
  • Bonus Blocks: Purchaseable starting at $100, increasing by 20% per block
  • Upkeep: $25 per chunk maintenance cost
  • Death Tax: When killed by another player, 5% of balance transferred (capped at $1000)
  • Jailing System: Outlawed players die in enemy territory → sent to prison
  • Jailing Costs: Jail fee = $500 + $100 per hour jailed

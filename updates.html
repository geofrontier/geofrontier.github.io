Update 0.9 - Shop

Date: 2025/01/19 | Author: ShilhohTony
  • Finished shop pricing and logistics for balanced gameplay
  • Hooked rank multipliers into shop pricing
  • Added shop events. Every so often, an event will occur what added a multiplier to specific categories
    • Argarian Festival (T2 - T1) will produce between 1.5x and 1.75x crop multiplier
    • Market surge (T2 - T1) will produce between 1.25x and 1.5x ore multiplier
  • Added dynamic pricing where for every x amount of an item sold, the price will drop by x%
    • Added as a way to nerf automatic farms
    • IE: 10,000 wheat sold = 10% reduction; 15,000 wheat sold = 25% reduction; ... ; 50,000 = Market Crash
  • Added logic for stacking multpliers

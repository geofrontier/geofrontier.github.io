1. Cheating and Exploiting

1.1 Unfair Gameplay Mods

Major - Severe

Clients, physical modifications, and resource packs that give you an unfair advantage in gameplay over others are forbidden.

Allowed Mods:

  • Performance Mods
  • Visual Mods
  • Sound Mods
  • Interface Mods / Minimaps (No entity radar)
  • Quality of Life Mods
  • Schematic Mods

Prohibited Mods:

  • Combat Cheats
  • ESP / X-Ray
  • Dupe Assisting
  • Hacked Clients
  • Movement Exploits
  • Automation & Macros (Excluding Schematics)

1.2 Exploiting Bugs / Glitches

Major - Severe

Intentionally taking advantage of modpack or server glitches or otherwise technical oversights is punishable. On the other hand, notifying staff of a bug / exploit may be rewardable.

1.3 Duplication

Major - Severe

You may not duplicate or find loopholes to generate an infinite or large amount of items, money, stats, etc.

Additional Penalty:

Economy wipe, ender chest wipe, & inventory wipe

1.4 Alternative Accounts

Moderate - Severe

Alternative accounts (AKA alts) are allowed unless they're used for malicious reasons.

Examples: Using an alt to steal, sabotage, using an alt to evade punishment, etc.

Additional Penalty:

2x punishment on all accounts

2. In-Game Behaviour - Chat

2.1 Chat Language

Minor - Severe

2.1-1 Respect and Civility: All members are expected to be respectful in their conduct. You are not allowed to:

  • Harass other members
  • Be involved in bigotry (including racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious prejudice and ableism)
  • Personal attacks against other members

2.1-2 Dehumanizing Language: To prevent dehumanizing remarks, you may not:

  • Encourage self-harm
  • Use slurs and derogatory terms to insult another
  • Spread hate speeches

2.1-3 Threats: You may not, under any circumstance, state cyber related threats including (but not limited to):

  • Exposure of Private Information
  • Personal Injury
  • Terrorism
  • DDoSing, or otherwise Hacking

Note: Basic swearing is fine

2.2 Spam

Minor - Severe

Disrupting chat by sending consistent messages fast, sending wall of texts, encouraging or provoking spam, and constantly repeating similar messages is not allowed.

2.3 Prohibited Topics

Minor - Severe

Discussion of controversial topics including politics and religion, or dark topics such as suicide, self-harm, or trauma is prohibited for the comfort of all members. It disrupts chat and leads to complications.

2.4 NSFW / NSFL

Minor - Severe

Absolutely no NSFW (Not Safe For Work) or NSFL (Not Safe For Life) content or topics in any form whatsoever. Crude and suggestive jokes are also disallowed.

2.5 Sharing Personal Information

Severe

You are prohibited from sharing the personal information of another at all without explicit permission for public use.

Examples: personal photos, age, address, social networks, relations, etc.

2.6 Advertising and Links

Moderate - Severe

Advertising another Minecraft server is forbidden. If you wish to share a server, you may private message the user on a social media platform.

Malicious links are not allowed, including links that: Log one's IP, download a virus, or lead to inappropriate content.

2.7 Respecting Authority

Minor - Major

The words / actions of a staff member are final. If a player has an issue with the decision, they may put in a complaint to a staff member of higher authority.

3. In-Game Behaviour - PvP

3.1 Camping

Minor - Major

Do not spawn kill players to the point where they're motivated to quit.

3.2 Griefing and Stealing

Minor - Severe

You are allowed to grief and / or steal against another player in the following situations:

  • In unclaimed territory / wilderness (all realms)
  • During a raid
  • During a war

Stealing during a consenting transaction / trade, against a member of the town / nation you're in or an ally is prohibited.

Collecting the loot off a legally killed player is not stealing, unless an arrangement was made beforehand to disallow taking loot.

3.3 Farming Stats

Minor - Severe

Repeatedly killing alternative accounts or players who agree to let you kill them to gain stats including a higher number of Kill points in raids is strictly prohibited.

Additional Penalty:

Loot rollback

4. General Nation Rules

4.1 Town and Nation Names

Minor - Moderate

If choosing a team name, keep it PG-13. A team's name cannot be the same or highly similar to another existing team without permission (This doesn't apply to historical regions your town or nation is primarily in).

Additional Penalty:

Town / nation removal

4.2 Group Discords

Moderate - Severe

Discord servers and group chats advertising a GeoFrontier town, nation or group must follow Discord's ToS and Community Guidelines as well as GeoFrontier's Rules.

This rule is only enforced in instances where said violations normally result in the termination of Discord account(s) or gameplay on our servers and occur in relatively public channels.

Examples: Doxxing, encouraging suicide, distributing hacked clients or illegal NSFW / NSFL content in general chats.

Additional Penalty:

TOS Break - town / nation wipe + ban wave

4.3 Claiming Patterns

4.3-1 Snaking: Claims cannot be less than 3 chunks wide when spanning over 3 chunks in length for longer than 72 hours. This does not apply to historical borders or panhandles created by natural terrain.

Penalty: Minor - Moderate + claim removal

4.3-2 Claim Blocking: Claims may not entirely enclose another claim without permission. Claiming an area to block a maximum of 2 cardinal directions (North, East, South, West) of another town / nation's claims is considered reasonable.

Penalty: Minor - Moderate + claim removal

4.3-3 Unclaimed Pockets: Claimed areas must be filled. There cannot be any enclosed wilderness chunks.

Penalty: Minor - Moderate + claim removal

4.3-4 Empty Outposts: Claimed outposts must have some kind of structure with purpose built within the core chunk.

Penalty: Minor - Moderate + claim removal

4.4 Traps

Minor - Moderate

Traps within claims may only be permitted if the chunk holding the trap has BREAK and PLACE flags set to TRUE for all parties.

Additional Penalty:

Loot rollback

6. Extra Rules

6.1 Sales

Moderate - Severe

Do not offer, sell, or buy in-game items, ranks, information or donator perks for real-world currency.

Do not charge back any official payments to GeoFrontier.

Chargeback Penalty:

Severe

6.2 Minecraft Accounts

Minecraft accounts may not have inappropriate usernames, nicknames or skins.

Minecraft accounts must additionally be legally bought by or gifted to the player as under Minecraft's Terms of Service.

Penalties:

[1] Mild - Ban until issues resolved

[2] Severe - Stolen account

6.3 Discord

Minor - Severe

Discord chat rules are equal to Chat rules (2.).

In voice chats, do not flood with music; there is alternative music to play. This rule applies once someone asks you to stop.

6.4 Adhere to Discord & Minecraft EULA

Severe

All members follow Discord's Terms of Service (including being 13+ or older depending on your region) and the Minecraft EULA and Terms of Service. Any violation will result in an immediate report and unappealable ban.

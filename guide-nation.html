Nation Quick Start

Create a Nation

  • /nation new [name] — $5,000
  • Need 5 claimed chunks in your town
  • Your town becomes the capital
  • Name: 3-20 chars, unique

Add Towns

  • /nation add [town] — invite
  • Target town types /nation accept
  • /nation kick [town] — remove
  • Can't kick capital

Set Relations

  • /nation ally [nation]
  • /nation enemy [nation]
  • /nation neutral [nation]
  • Other nation must accept

Nation Bank

  • /nation deposit [amt]
  • /nation withdraw [amt]
  • /nation set tax [amt]
  • Pays $1,000/day upkeep

1 Creating a Nation

Requirements to create a nation:

  • You must be the mayor or co-mayor of your town
  • Your town must have at least 5 claimed chunks
  • $5,000 in your town bank (not personal balance)
  • Nation name: 3-20 characters, must be unique
/nation new YourNation Creates nation with your town as capital

What happens next: Your town becomes the capital. You're now the king/queen. You can immediately invite other towns to join.

Tip: Pick a name that reflects your nation's theme (geographic, historical, or ideological). You can see existing nations with /nation list.

2 Managing Member Towns

Inviting towns: Target town must be willing to join. They keep their mayor, town bank, and autonomy — they just align with you politically.

/nation add TownName Sends invitation
/nation kick TownName Removes a town (can't kick capital)
/nation set capital TownName Transfer capital to another member

Member benefits: Nation chat (/nc), shared borders (can claim adjacent), collective defense, claim bonuses.

Recruitment strategy: Look for towns geographically close to you — easier to defend and coordinate. Check /town list to see nearby towns.

3 Diplomacy & Relations

You can set relations with other nations. Both sides must agree (except enemy declarations).

/nation ally NationName Propose alliance
/nation enemy NationName Declare as enemy (immediate)
/nation neutral NationName Set to neutral

What each relation means:

  • Ally: Can't attack each other's towns, share borders, can use each other's spawns ($5 cost)
  • Neutral: No special interaction, can still attack in wilderness
  • Enemy: Can attack anywhere, war can be declared

Warning: Breaking an alliance damages your reputation. Other nations will be less likely to trust you.

4 Warfare & Defense

To declare war: Must be enemies first, then declare.

/nation declarewar NationName Start war (24h notice)
/nation peace NationName Propose peace

War mechanics:

  • 24-hour notice period before fighting starts
  • During war, you can attack enemy members anywhere (including their towns)
  • Killing enemies in their towns costs them more (jail time, higher death fees)
  • War ends when one side surrenders or negotiates peace
  • 7-day truce after war ends (can't re-declare)

Defense: Fortify border towns, set nation spawn in a safe location (/nation set spawn), coordinate with allies.

5 Economy & Taxes

Daily upkeep: $1,000 per day for the nation itself (not per town). Paid from nation bank at midnight EST.

/nation set tax [amount] Tax per town per day (max $500)
/nation deposit [amount] Add money to nation bank
/nation withdraw [amount] Remove money (use carefully)

Nation bank is separate from town banks. Money in nation bank can only be used for:

  • Paying nation upkeep
  • Funding wars (bounties, rewards)
  • Building nation projects

Bankruptcy: If nation bank can't pay upkeep, member towns will automatically leave one by one until costs are covered. Keep at least $7,000 buffer.

6 Government & Ranks

Assign roles to trusted players in your nation:

/nation rank add [player] [rank] Assign a rank
/nation rank remove [player] [rank] Remove rank

Available ranks:

  • chancellor: Can manage finances, invite/kick towns, set taxes
  • general: Can declare war, manage enemies
  • ambassador: Can manage alliances and relations

King/Queen: The nation creator or whoever holds the capital. Has ALL permissions including disbanding nation.

7 Territory & Expansion

Nations don't claim land directly — your towns do. But being in a nation affects claiming:

  • Shared borders: Towns in same nation can claim directly adjacent (ignores 4-chunk buffer)
  • Nation bonus claims: Each town gets extra claim blocks based on nation size
  • Nation spawn: Set a central hub with /nation set spawn (capital town only)

Nation claim bonuses (added to each town's limit):

  • 3-5 towns: +50 claim blocks
  • 6-10 towns: +150 claim blocks
  • 11+ towns: +300 claim blocks

8 Useful Commands

/nation View nation info
/nation list List all nations
/nation spawn Teleport to nation spawn ($5, 30s)
/nation online See who's online in your nation
/nation set board [msg] Set nation message board
/nation set tag [3-4 chars] Set nation tag (shows in chat)
/nation rename [new name] Rename nation ($10,000)

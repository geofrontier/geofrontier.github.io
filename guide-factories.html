Factories Guide

Craft weapons, ammunition, and artillery for your nation's military-industrial complex

Get Started Movecraft Guide →

1 Creating a Factory

Factories are specialized crafting stations that allow mass production of weapons and ammunition. Each factory is tied to a specific chest and region.

/factory new (Factory Type) Creates a new factory while looking at a chest

Example: To create an Armory factory for making muskets and swords:
/factory new Armory
Must be executed while looking directly at the chest that will become the factory.

Important: By default, players cannot run this command! You must apply for factory registration first (see Section 2).

Available Factory Types:

  • Armory: Produces flintlock weapons and melee weapons
  • Foundry: Produces artillery pieces (cannons, mortars, culverins)
  • Munitions: Produces ammunition (cannonballs, gunpowder barrels)

2 Factory Registration

All factories must be approved by server staff to prevent abuse and ensure proper integration with the server economy.

Step 1: Build

Construct a proper factory building around your chest. This should be a themed structure (blacksmith, armory, etc.)

Step 2: Apply

Go to Discord channel #factory-apply and post your factory coordinates + screenshots of the build

Step 3: Approval

Staff will review your application. Only factories with decent or okay buildings will be approved

Step 4: Region

Once approved, staff will create a factory region around your build where the factory commands will work

Building Tips: Your factory building should be themed appropriately (blacksmith for armory, industrial building for foundry, etc.). Include appropriate decorations like anvils, furnaces, workbenches, and storage areas.

Region Restriction: Factory commands only work inside the factory region created by staff. You cannot use factory commands elsewhere, even if you own the factory chest.

3 Using Your Factory

Once your factory is registered and you're inside its region, you can begin production.

/factory activate (Recipe Name) Starts production of a specific item
/factory list Shows all recipes available in your factory
/factory info Shows factory status, fuel, and production queue

Production Process:

  1. Place required raw materials in the factory chest
  2. Use /factory activate (recipe) while looking at the chest
  3. Factory consumes materials and begins production
  4. Finished product appears in the chest after processing time
  5. Some factories require fuel (coal, charcoal) for operation

Efficiency Tip: Keep your factory chest stocked with common materials. Production is faster when all materials are pre-loaded. Consider creating automated supply lines from your storage rooms.

Each factory type produces specific items. Here are all available recipes:

Recipe Names: Use the exact recipe names shown above (lowercase with underscores) when activating production. For example: /factory activate musket or /factory activate gunpowder_barrel.

5 Factory Sharing & Permissions

Manage who can use your factory and coordinate production within your nation.

/factory addmember (PlayerName) Allows another player to use your factory
/factory removemember (PlayerName) Removes a player's access to your factory
/factory transfer (PlayerName) Transfers factory ownership to another player

Factory Economy:

  • Nation Factories: Consider setting up dedicated factory workers within your nation
  • Resource Allocation: Coordinate mining operations to supply your factories
  • Trade: Excess production can be sold to other nations (within server rules)
  • Strategic Reserve: Maintain emergency stockpiles for wartime production surges

Security: Factory regions are not automatically protected from non-members. Use Towny permissions or physical security to prevent unauthorized access to your factory chest.

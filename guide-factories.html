1 Factories & extraction sites: how they work

On GeoFrontier, factories and extraction sites are the backbone of your town's economy. They allow you to convert raw materials into useful goods (factories) or passively generate resources from the environment (extraction sites). Both systems require a staff‑reviewed application and a properly constructed building.

Shared principles:

  • You must apply on Discord – staff will review your build and activate the site.
  • Every site requires a building of at least 6 chunks total volume (not just a 6‑chunk width – the entire structure must occupy the space of six chunks).
  • The building must be visually distinguishable as a factory or an extraction site – a simple hole in the ground or a single chest will not be accepted.
  • All commands are chest‑based: you must look directly at the chest that serves as the site's interface when typing /factory create or /extract create.
  • Extraction sites are currently enabled; basic factories (armory, foundry, munitions) are enabled; siege and wagon factories are currently disabled.
Key difference: Factories consume materials to produce other items; extraction sites passively generate ores, wood, or crops over time.

2 Applying for a factory or extraction site

You cannot simply place a chest and run a command – every industrial site must be reviewed and approved by staff. This ensures that all factories and extraction sites are properly integrated into the world and meet the server's quality standards.

Step‑by‑step application

  1. Build your structure – construct a building that meets the size and appearance requirements (see section 3).
  2. Take screenshots – capture the exterior and interior, showing the chest that will serve as the factory/extraction interface.
  3. Open a ticket in Discord – go to the #support channel and open a ticket with the title “Factory application” or “Extraction site application”.
  4. Provide coordinates and screenshots – staff will visit your town to inspect the build.
  5. Staff approval – if the build is acceptable, staff will activate the site. You will receive a confirmation message.
Important: Unapproved sites will not function – attempting to use commands on an unapproved chest will do nothing. Do not build a factory or extraction site without applying first; it will be removed.

3 Building requirements (size & appearance)

To maintain immersion and fairness, every factory or extraction site must be housed in a proper building. The rules are the same for both types.

Size requirement

The building must occupy at least 6 chunks in total volume. This does not mean 6 chunks in a single dimension – it means the three‑dimensional space of six chunks. For example:

  • A building 3 chunks long × 2 chunks wide × 1 chunk high (3×2×1 = 6 chunks) meets the requirement.
  • A tall tower 2×2×2 (8 chunks) also qualifies.
  • A small shack (1×1×1) does not.

Appearance requirement

The building must be visually distinguishable as an industrial or extractive facility. Staff look for:

  • Factories: Smokestacks, large furnaces, anvils, mechanical details, storage lofts, conveyor‑like designs.
  • Extraction sites: Mine entrances with supports, quarry pits with scaffolding, lumber camps with sawmills, farm complexes with silos and irrigation.

A simple wooden box with a chest inside will be rejected. Take inspiration from real‑world industrial buildings – show that you put effort into the design.

Tip: Use a variety of blocks (wood, stone, metal) and add details like trapdoors, fences, and slabs to give your building character. Label the chest with a sign (e.g. “Armory” or “Quarry Output”).

4 Extraction sites – passive resource generation

Extraction sites do not require input materials. Instead, they passively produce resources over time. You must create the site, then toggle it on.

Types of extraction sites, costs, and production times

Quarry

Creation cost: $10,000

Extraction time: 600s (10 min)

Radius: 16 blocks

Produces stone, ores, and related minerals.

Logging Camp

Creation cost: $8,000

Extraction time: 300s (5 min)

Radius: 24 blocks

Produces various logs, sticks, apples, saplings.

Farm

Creation cost: $5,000

Extraction time: 300s (5 min)

Radius: 20 blocks

Produces crops and melons.

Mining Outpost

Creation cost: $15,000

Extraction time: 900s (15 min)

Radius: 12 blocks

Rich ore extraction – iron, gold, redstone, even diamonds.

Creating and operating an extraction site

Once your building is approved, use the following commands while looking at the chest that will serve as the output container:

/extract create quarry
Creates a quarry extraction site ($10,000).
/extract create logging_camp
Creates a logging camp ($8,000).
/extract create farm
Creates a farm ($5,000).
/extract create mining_outpost
Creates a mining outpost ($15,000).
/extract toggle
Turns the site on or off. When on, resources will appear in the chest at regular intervals.

Example workflow – mining outpost:

  1. Build a mining outpost building (≥6 chunks, looks like a mine head).
  2. Apply on Discord, get approved.
  3. Stand in front of your chest and type /extract create mining_outpost ($15,000 deducted).
  4. Type /extract toggle to start production.
  5. After 15 minutes, ores appear in the chest – up to 20 iron, 24 coal, and occasionally diamonds.
Tip: Extraction sites work best when placed in suitable biomes – farms on plains, quarries in mountains, logging camps in forests. The radius gives a rough idea of the area the site “works” – build your structure accordingly.

5 Factories – types, creation costs, and recipes

Factories consume materials from their chest to produce advanced items. Each factory type has a specific creation cost (paid from your town bank) and a list of items it can produce. All commands are performed while looking at the factory chest.

Factory types and creation costs

Armory

Creation cost: $5,000 | Base production time: 60s

Produces flintlock firearms, melee weapons, and tools.

Foundry

Creation cost: $10,000 | Base production time: 120s

Produces artillery pieces (cannons, mortars, culverins).

Munitions

Creation cost: $3,000 | Base production time: 30s

Produces ammunition (cannonballs, gunpowder barrels).

Siege Works

Creation cost: $15,000 | Currently disabled

Would produce siege equipment (ballistae, catapults, trebuchets).

Wagon Works

Creation cost: $8,000 | Currently disabled

Would produce wagons (conestoga, armored, royal).

Creating a factory (chest-based)

/factory create armory|foundry|munitions|siege_works|wagon_works
Creates a factory of the specified type (cost deducted from town bank). Note: siege_works and wagon_works are disabled.
/factory produce <item>
Starts production of the given item. Requires ingredients in the chest.
/factory stop
Halts current production (materials are not refunded).
/factory remove
Deletes the factory (use only if you intend to replace it).

Example: producing a musket

  1. Build an armory building (≥6 chunks, looks like a weapons workshop).
  2. Apply on Discord, get approved.
  3. Stand in front of your chest and type /factory create armory ($5,000 deducted).
  4. Place 5 Iron Ingot, 2 Stick, and 1 Flint in the chest.
  5. Type /factory produce musket while looking at the chest.
  6. After 60 seconds, a musket appears in the chest.
Note: Siege and wagon factories are currently disabled (vehicles-enabled: false, siege-factories-enabled: false). Only armory, foundry, and munitions are operational.

6 Command reference (all chest‑based)

All commands require you to be looking directly at the chest that serves as the site's interface. If you are not looking at a valid, approved chest, the command will fail.

Extraction sites

/extract create quarry
Creates a quarry ($10,000).
/extract create logging_camp
Creates a logging camp ($8,000).
/extract create farm
Creates a farm ($5,000).
/extract create mining_outpost
Creates a mining outpost ($15,000).
/extract toggle
Starts or stops passive generation.

Factories

/factory create armory|foundry|munitions|siege_works|wagon_works
Creates a factory of the given type (siege_works/wagon_works disabled).
/factory produce <item>
Begins production of the specified item (requires materials in chest).
/factory stop
Stops current production.
/factory remove
Deletes the factory.
Remember: None of these commands will work until your build has been approved by staff. Plan your construction carefully, apply, and then start producing.
Building size reminder: The structure must occupy at least 6 chunks total (volume) and look the part – a chest in a 2×2 hut will be rejected.

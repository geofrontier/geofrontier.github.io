Factory System

Factories are specialized production facilities that allow mass crafting of weapons, ammunition, artillery, and siege equipment for your nation's military and economy.

Armory

Produces firearms and melee weapons

Cost: $5,000
Production: 60s

Foundry

Produces artillery pieces

Cost: $10,000
Production: 120s

Munitions

Produces ammunition

Cost: $3,000
Production: 30s

Siege Works

Produces siege equipment

Cost: $15,000
Production: 180s

Wagon Works

Produces wagons & transports

Cost: $8,000
Production: 150s

Important: All factory commands must be executed while looking at the factory chest. Stand close to the chest and face it when running factory commands.

1 Creating Factories

Factories are created from chests that become specialized production stations for your town. Each factory type has different costs and production times.

/factory create <type> Creates a factory from the chest you're looking at. Must be looking at the chest!

Example: To create an Armory factory for making muskets and swords:
/factory create armory
Costs $5,000 from your town bank. Production time: 60 seconds per item.

/factory remove Removes/deletes the factory you're looking at

Important: All factories must be approved by admins before they become functional. Factories cannot be chests laying in the open - they must be inside proper factory buildings (blacksmith, armory, etc.). See Section 5 for building requirements.

Factory Placement: Stand directly in front of the chest you want to turn into a factory. Look directly at it and make sure you're within 5 blocks. The chest will become the factory interface for that production type.

2 Factory Types & Costs

Each factory type specializes in different products and has unique creation costs and production times.

Factory Type Creation Cost Production Time Specialization
armory $5,000 60 seconds Firearms & melee weapons (muskets, swords, etc.)
foundry $10,000 120 seconds Artillery pieces (cannons, mortars, culverins)
munitions $3,000 30 seconds Ammunition (cannonballs, gunpowder barrels)
siege_works $15,000 180 seconds Siege equipment (battering rams, siege towers, etc.)
wagon_works $8,000 150 seconds Wagons & transport vehicles

Cost Management: Factory creation costs are deducted from your town bank. Ensure your town has sufficient funds before attempting to create factories. Production times are per item - plan your military production accordingly.

Type Limitations: Certain items can only be produced in specific factory types. For example, maces can only be made in armories, while cannons can only be made in foundries. Use /factory recipes <item> to check where an item can be produced.

3 Factory Commands

Once your factory is built and approved, use these commands to manage production.

/factory produce <item> Starts production of a specific item. Must be looking at the factory chest!

Production Example:
1. Look at your Armory factory chest
2. Type: /factory produce musket
3. Factory checks for 5 Iron Ingot, 2 Stick, 1 Flint in the chest
4. If materials are present, begins 60-second production of muskets

/factory recipes <item> Shows the recipe for a specific item and which factory type can produce it

Recipe Check Example:
/factory recipes cannon
Shows: "Cannon recipe: 8 Iron Block, 4 Iron Ingot, 4 Oak Log - Can be produced in: foundry"

/factory stop Stops production in the factory you're looking at

Production Process:

  1. Place required raw materials in the factory chest
  2. Use /factory produce <item> while looking at the chest
  3. Factory consumes materials and begins production timer (60s, 120s, etc.)
  4. When timer completes, produced items appear in the chest
  5. Use /factory stop to halt production if needed

Production Strategy: Keep your factory chests stocked with common materials. For efficient production, create supply chains from mining operations to your factories. Use /factory recipes to plan resource gathering.

5 Rules & Limits

Follow these rules to ensure fair factory usage and avoid penalties.

Building Requirements

Factories cannot be chests laying in the open. Must be inside proper factory buildings (blacksmith, armory, etc.)

Factory Limits

Only 14 factory chests per town including all types. First offense: warning + deletion. Second: treasury wipe + blacklist.

Bug Abuse

Trying to dupe or exploit factory bugs = 2 nation warnings + half of nation's treasury wiped.

Placement Rules

You may NOT place a factory on a ship. Factories must be in permanent structures.

Good Factory Layout:

  • Armory: Inside a blacksmith building with anvils, furnaces, weapon racks
  • Foundry: Industrial building with blast furnaces, lava cauldrons, molds
  • Munitions: Powder magazine with barrels, sandbags, safety signs
  • Siege Works: Workshop with cranes, winches, large construction areas
  • Wagon Works: Carpenter shop with wood piles, wheels, tools
  • Label factories with signs for better organization (recommended for town rankings)

Penalty System:

  • Over 14 factories: First offense = warning + deletion of extra factories. Second offense = half of treasury wiped + national blacklist
  • Bug Abuse: 2 nation warnings + half of nation's treasury wiped
  • Poor Factory Buildings: Factory destroyed + town warning
  • Factory on Ship: Immediate deletion + warning

Approval Process: After building your factory structure, you must apply for approval in the Discord #factory-apply channel. Post coordinates + screenshots. Only properly built factories will be approved and made functional by staff.

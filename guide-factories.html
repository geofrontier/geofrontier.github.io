Factory System

Factories are specialized production facilities that allow mass crafting of weapons, ammunition, and resources for your nation's military and economy.

Armory

Produces flintlock weapons and melee weapons

Foundry

Produces artillery pieces (cannons, mortars, culverins)

Munitions

Produces ammunition (cannonballs, gunpowder barrels)

Passive Factories

Quarry, Mining Camp, Logging Camp, Farm - generate resources passively

Production Strategy: Factories work as chests that process materials. Place required resources in the factory chest, use the production command, and items will be crafted automatically until resources run out.

1 Creating Factories

Factories are created from chests that become specialized production stations for your town.

/Factory Create (Factory Type) Creates a factory from the chest you're looking at

Example: To create an Armory factory for making muskets and swords:
/Factory Create Armory
Must be executed while looking directly at the chest that will become the factory.

/Produce (Recipe Name) Starts production using resources in the factory chest

Production Example:
1. Look at your Armory factory chest
2. Type: /Produce musket
3. Factory checks for 5 Iron Ingot, 2 Stick, 1 Flint in the chest
4. If materials are present, produces muskets until resources run out

Important: All factories must be approved by admins before they become functional. See Section 2 for approval requirements.

2 Factory Approval Rules

All factories must meet building requirements and be approved by server staff to prevent abuse and maintain server quality.

Rule 1: Proper Buildings

Factories cannot be chests laying in the open. They must be inside proper factory buildings (blacksmith, armory, etc.)

Rule 2: Passive Factories

Quarry, Mining Camp, Logging Camp, and Farm factories work differently - they don't require resources and passively generate materials

Rule 3: Example Builds

Check the server forums for examples of proper factory buildings. Failure to build properly = factory destroyed + town warning

Rule 4: Labeling

Factories should be labeled (optional but recommended for town rankings)

Bug Abuse Warning: Trying to purposely find bugs or trying to dupe and bug out the muskets/guns will result in 2 nation warnings and half of the nation's treasury being wiped.

3 Using Factories

Once your factory is built and approved, you can begin production of weapons and resources.

Production Process:

  1. Place required raw materials in the factory chest
  2. Use /Produce (recipe name) while looking at the chest
  3. Factory consumes materials and produces items automatically
  4. Production continues until resources run out
  5. Passive factories (quarry, mining camp, etc.) generate resources automatically without input

Efficiency Tip: Keep your factory chests stocked with common materials. For passive factories, ensure they're placed in appropriate locations (quarries near stone, logging camps in forests, etc.).

Passive Factory Example: A Mining Camp factory placed underground will passively generate ores. A Logging Camp in a forest will generate wood. These require no resources, just proper placement.

Each factory type produces specific items. Here are all available recipes:

cannon: 8 Iron Block, 4 Iron Ingot, 4 Oak Log

Melee Weapons (Armory Factory)

dagger: 2 Iron Ingot, 1 Stick
cutlass: 3 Iron Ingot, 1 Stick
cavalry_sword: 2 Diamond, 1 Iron Ingot, 1 Stick

Recipe Names: Use the exact recipe names shown above (lowercase with underscores) when producing. For example: /Produce musket or /Produce gunpowder_barrel.

5 Rules & Limits

Follow these rules to ensure fair factory usage and avoid penalties.

Factory Limits: Only 14 factory chests per town including quarries, mining camps, farms, and logging camps.

Penalty System:

  • First Offense (Over 14 factories): Warning and deletion of extra factories
  • Second Offense: Half of treasury wiped + national blacklist
  • Bug Abuse: 2 nation warnings + half of nation's treasury wiped
  • Poor Factory Buildings: Factory destroyed + town warning

Factory Placement:

  • You may NOT place a factory on a ship
  • Factories must be in proper buildings (not open chests)
  • Label factories for better organization (recommended for rankings)
  • Passive factories should be placed in appropriate biomes/locations

Good Factory Layout:

  • Armory: Inside a blacksmith building with anvils, furnaces
  • Foundry: Industrial building with blast furnaces, lava cauldrons
  • Quarry: Near stone/mountains with mining equipment decorations
  • Logging Camp: In forest with wood piles, sawmills

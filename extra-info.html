Banned items & mechanics

  • ⚔️ Maces
    • Mace (weapon)
    • Heavy core – warning only (cannot be crafted into mace)
  • 🍎 Golden apples
    • Golden apple
    • Enchanted golden apple (god apple)
  • 🛶 All regular wooden boats & rafts
    • All boat types: oak, spruce, birch, jungle, acacia, dark oak, mangrove, cherry
    • Bamboo raft
    • All chest boat variants (oak chest boat, spruce chest boat, … , bamboo chest raft)
  • 👁️ Ender items
    • Ender chests
    • Ender pearls
    • Eyes of ender
  • 🔨 Netherite tools & armour
    • Netherite helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots
    • Netherite sword, pickaxe, axe, shovel, hoe
    • Netherite ingot – warning only
  • 🔥 Nether as a whole
    • The Nether dimension is completely disabled
    • Portals cannot be lit – any portal blocks are automatically removed
  • 💎 Beacons
    • Beacon block (cannot be placed, removed from inventory)
    • Nether star – warning only (cannot be crafted into beacon)
    • Wither skeleton skull – warning only
Allowed: Diamond tools/armor, enchanting tables, nether stars (with warning).
Server message on join: “diamond allowed, boats/chestboats/rafts banned, netherite banned, enchanting tables unbanned, ender pearls banned, offhand enforced, nether disabled”

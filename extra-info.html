⛔ Nether & Portals

The Nether is completely disabled – any portal attempt is cancelled and nearby portal blocks are removed.
  • Portal ignition – cancelled, player receives nether_msg: &cThe Nether is disabled on this server!
  • Existing portals – automatically deleted within 8 blocks of any player every 5 seconds.
  • Flint and steel / fire charges – can still be used, but any formed portal is immediately nuked.
[Skript] bannedItems.sk running (diamond allowed, boats/chestboats/rafts banned, netherite banned, enchanting tables unbanned, ender pearls banned, offhand enforced, nether disabled)

⚔️ Netherite Items (all banned)

Any netherite item – armor, tools, weapons – is removed from inventory on join, inventory close, item hold change, container open, or drop. Netherite ingots also trigger a warning.

Armor

  • Netherite helmet
  • Netherite chestplate
  • Netherite leggings
  • Netherite boots

Weapons & Tools

  • Netherite sword
  • Netherite pickaxe
  • Netherite axe
  • Netherite shovel
  • Netherite hoe

Material

  • Netherite ingot (warning only, not auto‑removed)

Note: Diamond items are explicitly allowed (see console log).

🛶 Boats & Rafts (all banned)

Every variant of boat, chest boat, and bamboo raft is prohibited. Removed from inventories, containers, and on drop.

Standard boats

  • Oak boat
  • Spruce boat
  • Birch boat
  • Jungle boat
  • Acacia boat
  • Dark oak boat
  • Mangrove boat
  • Cherry boat
  • Bamboo raft

Chest boats

  • Oak chest boat
  • Spruce chest boat
  • Birch chest boat
  • Jungle chest boat
  • Acacia chest boat
  • Dark oak chest boat
  • Mangrove chest boat
  • Cherry chest boat
  • Bamboo chest raft

🍎 Golden Apples

Both regular and enchanted golden apples are banned. Removed on sight.

  • Golden apple
  • Enchanted golden apple (God apple)

👁️ Ender Items (all banned)

Ender pearls, eyes of ender, and ender chests are prohibited. They are stripped from inventories, containers, and cannot be placed.

  • Ender pearl
  • Ender eye (Eye of Ender)
  • Ender chest (placement cancelled, block set to air)
Offhand slot is monitored – any banned item placed there is immediately removed.

🔨 Mace & Heavy Core

  • Mace – fully banned (removed from all inventories).
  • Heavy core – not auto‑removed, but holding it shows a warning:
    &eMaces are banned on this server &7(Heavy Core can't be crafted into a Mace)&e.

💎 Beacons & Nether Stars

  • Beacon – banned. Removed from inventories; placement cancelled and block set to air.
  • Nether star – NOT removed, but holding it triggers a warning:
    &eBeacons are banned on this server &7(Nether Star can't be crafted into a Beacon)&e.
  • Wither skeleton skull – holding it gives a warning: &eBeacons are banned on this server!

⚙️ Enforcement mechanics

On join
Full inventory scan (including main hand & offhand) – banned items removed, action bar: &cBanned item removed!
On inventory close
Scans whole inventory + offhand, removes banned items.
On item held change
Checks new main hand and offhand – removes banned, shows warnings for heavy core/nether star/wither skull.
On inventory open (chest etc.)
Scans the container's contents and removes any banned items.
On item spawn (drop)
Dropped banned items are killed (removed) immediately.
On block place
Beacon and ender chest placement are cancelled, block set to air, action bar sent.
Offhand enforcement: Any banned item placed in the offhand is deleted, regardless of how it got there.

Enchanting tables are explicitly allowed (per console log).

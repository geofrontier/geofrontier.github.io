Dangerous Seas

"The sea does not forgive. The sea does not forget. Build with iron and stone, or join the drowned."

Danger Zones - Distance From Shore

Shore Waters (0-10 blocks)

  • Mild danger only
  • Can swim briefly (max 100 blocks total)
  • Some creature spawns

Coastal Waters (10-30 blocks)

  • Creatures begin hunting
  • Waves and riptides active
  • Swimming becomes suicidal

Deep Ocean (30-75 blocks)

  • Sharks & hostile creatures
  • Storms and whirlpools
  • Hypothermia (starts at 60s)

The Abyss (75+ blocks)

  • Instant death if swimming
  • Kraken territory
  • Maximum danger multiplier

Critical Dangers

Swimming

Maximum 100 blocks before exhaustion. Damage when exhausted. Instant death in deep ocean. Use ships only!

Hypothermia

Begins after 60 seconds in water. Causes damage and slowness. Can freeze you solid at high severity.

Storms

25% chance to spawn. Last 5 minutes. Destroy wooden ships. Lightning causes fires. Rogue waves knock players overboard.

Sea Creatures

Sharks, Sea Terrors, Sirens, Leviathan, and Kraken. Attracted by blood in water. 2.5x more at night.

⚠️ Your boat is taking heavy damage! (80% & 93%)
Player bleeding out in a vortex - Blood attracts predators!

Blood in Water

Taking damage attracts predators! Sharks have 10x attraction to blood. Other creatures 3x. Blood scent lasts 60 seconds.

Ship Survival - Your ONLY Hope

WOODEN SHIPS WILL BE DESTROYED

All wood types, glass, wool, torches, chests, and decorative blocks are VULNERABLE and will break in storms. Boats capsize and break instantly in open ocean.

Hull Breaches & Flooding

Ships can spring leaks during storms. Water spreads quickly (speed: 3). Damage every 30 ticks when flooding. Keep repair materials on board.

Ocean Hazards

Whirlpools

5% spawn chance. 20 block radius. Inescapable pull (strength: 1.2). 2.0 damage per second.

Water Pressure

Below 15 blocks depth. 0.1 damage per additional block. Deep water crushes the unprepared.

Fog

20% chance. Lasts 80 seconds. Causes blindness. Navigate by compass or wait it out.

Night Multipliers

All dangers 2.0x worse at night. Creatures 2.5x more common. Reduced visibility.

Death at Sea - Consequences

Permanent Loss

Items sink and are lost forever when drowning. 100% experience loss. Respawn with 5 minutes of weakness.

Survival Strategy

1. Never swim in open ocean. 2. Build ships with metal/stone. 3. Avoid storms when possible. 4. Don't travel at night. 5. Keep repair materials on board. 6. Have emergency supplies.

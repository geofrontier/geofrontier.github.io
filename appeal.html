1 Before You Appeal

Before submitting an appeal, please understand our punishment philosophy and the requirements for a successful appeal.

Important Notes: We do not ban or mute players without proof. This appeal form is for players who feel they have changed and deserve a second chance, or who believe their punishment was unfair in the first place.

Reasons to Appeal:

  • You've genuinely changed: You understand what you did wrong and have grown from the experience
  • Unfair punishment: You believe there was a misunderstanding or error in your punishment
  • New evidence: You have evidence that wasn't considered during the original decision
  • Appropriate waiting period: You've waited the required time before appealing (7 days for permanent mutes/bans)

Honesty is Key: To save everyone's time, if you lie in your appeal, we will automatically reject it and may extend your punishment. If you are respectful and understanding in your appeal, we are more likely to shorten the punishment as a thank you for your maturity.

2 Appeal Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to create an effective appeal that will be seriously considered by our staff team.

Be Respectful

Address staff politely. Angry or demanding appeals are less likely to succeed.

Be Honest

Admit mistakes if you made them. We have punishment logs and can verify claims.

Provide Context

Explain what happened from your perspective and why the punishment should be lifted/shortened.

Wait Your Turn

Please wait 7 days between appeals. Spamming appeals will result in extended punishment.

Critical Rules:

  • No harassment: Do not harass staff regarding the status of your appeal. This will extend your punishment.
  • No false claims: We keep detailed punishment logs and can verify all claims made in appeals.
  • Respect decisions: If your appeal is rejected, wait 7 days before appealing again.
  • One appeal at a time: Submit only one appeal and wait for a response.

Effective Appeal Structure:
1. Acknowledge what you did wrong (if applicable)
2. Explain any context or misunderstanding
3. Describe how you've changed or what you've learned
4. Provide any evidence supporting your case
5. Politely request reconsideration

3 Appeal Process

Understand what happens after you submit your appeal and what to expect from our staff team.

Step 1: Submission

Complete the appeal form with all required information and submit it.

Step 2: Processing

It may take up to an hour for your appeal to be forwarded to our staff team.

Step 3: Review

Volunteer staff will review your appeal on their free time. This requires patience.

Step 4: Decision

You will receive a decision. Note that we may shorten rather than fully lift a punishment.

Timeline Expectations: Our staff are volunteers who review appeals on their free time. Please be patient and do not reach out directly regarding the status of your application. If your punishment has not been lifted within 3 days, assume it has been rejected.

No Status Updates: We do not email applicants about the status of their appeal. Repeated inquiries about appeal status may result in your appeal being rejected or your punishment being extended.

Possible Outcomes:

  • Appeal Accepted: Your punishment is lifted entirely
  • Appeal Partially Accepted: Your punishment is shortened but not fully lifted
  • Appeal Rejected: Your punishment remains unchanged
  • Appeal Rejected with Extension: Your punishment is extended due to dishonesty or rule violations in the appeal process

4 Appeal Form

Use the form below to submit your appeal. Please fill out all required fields completely and honestly.

Form Tips:

  • Have your evidence ready before starting the form (Google Drive or YouTube links preferred)
  • Be specific about when you were punished (approximately how long ago)
  • Provide your exact username on the affected platform
  • Write your explanation in a separate document first, then paste it into the form
  • Double-check all information before submitting

5 Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the appeal process and their answers.

How long does it take to get a response to my appeal?

It may take up to an hour for your appeal to be forwarded to staff, and then additional time for them to review it on their free time. If your punishment has not been lifted within 3 days, assume it has been rejected.

Can I appeal a permanent ban or mute?

Yes, but you must wait 7 days from a permanent mute/ban before appealing. This gives you time to reflect and allows emotions to settle.

What happens if I lie in my appeal?

If you lie in your appeal, we will automatically reject it and may extend your punishment. We keep detailed punishment logs and can verify claims.

Can I appeal more than once?

Yes, but you must wait 7 days between appeals. If you appeal before the 7-day waiting period, it will be automatically rejected.

What kind of evidence should I provide?

Screenshots, video recordings, or chat logs that support your case. Google Drive or YouTube links are preferred for sharing evidence.

Will my punishment definitely be lifted if I appeal?

Not necessarily. We may choose to shorten rather than fully lift a punishment. The outcome depends on the severity of the offense, your attitude in the appeal, and the evidence provided.

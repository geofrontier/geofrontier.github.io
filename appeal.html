Appeal a Punishment

Submit an appeal for a second chance or to contest a punishment

Important Notes

We do not ban/mute without proof. This appeal form is if you feel you have changed and deserve a second chance, or if you feel your punishment was unfair.

To save everyone's time: If you lie, we will automatically reject and extend the punishment. If you are respectful and understanding, we are more likely to shorten the punishment.

Please wait 7 days from appeals before re-appealing. If you spam these appeals or harass staff regarding the status, we will extend the punishment. For permanent mutes/bans, wait 7 days before appealing.

We keep detailed punishment logs and can verify all claims made in appeals. Honesty and respect go a long way in the appeal process.

Appeal Timeline & Process

  • Submission: Complete and submit the form below
  • Processing: May take up to an hour to be forwarded to staff
  • Review: Volunteer staff review appeals on their free time
  • No Status Updates: We do not email applicants about appeal status
  • Decision: If punishment not lifted within 3 days, assume rejection
  • Possible Outcomes: Punishment may be shortened rather than fully lifted
